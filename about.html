About EverGleam

EverGleam crafts timeless silver jewellery designed for everyday elegance. We blend classic artistry with modern design to create pieces that shine for years.

What we stand for

  • Pure 925 Silver: Hallmarked quality with skin-friendly plating.
  • Golden Jewellery: High-quality gold-plated designs that give a rich look.
  • Fair Pricing: Direct-to-you pricing without middlemen markups.
  • Care & Warranty: Free polishing within 6 months and hassle-free returns.

Our Story

Started by jewellery lovers and engineers, EverGleam began as a small studio in Mumbai. Today, we ship across India while staying true to hand-finished craft and strict quality checks.
